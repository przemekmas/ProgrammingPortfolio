About Me


My name is Przemyslaw Maslowski, and I have developed an interest in computers at the age of 13 when I have built my first gaming computer. After building computers, I knew that I wanted to pursue a career in IT. Therefore, I have decided to study computer science for my Bachelor's degree. I have enjoyed various topics throughout my studies at the university. However, I liked the programming units the most, and I knew that I wanted to pursue my career in programming. The first programming languages that I learned were Java and Python. I have also learned C# later throughout my studies, and it was the language that I enjoyed the most. After graduating with a first-class degree from the University of Bedfordshire, I have secured a job as a Junior Software Developer. I have developed desktop-based applications and services with TSQL, C#, WPF, WinForms, and Xamarin. I was quickly promoted to an Intermediate Software Developer, and I have developed good relationships with clients. After four years of developing desktop applications, I have secured a job as a Software Engineer and started developing web-based applications.
